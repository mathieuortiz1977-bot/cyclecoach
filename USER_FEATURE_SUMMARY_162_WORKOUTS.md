## Real-World Example

### Your Training Goal: Build VO2 Max

**Scientifically Optimal Protocol Rotation:**

1. **Seiler 4×8** — Research shows this is peak VO2max stimulus
   - 4 intervals of 8 minutes at 105-110% FTP
   - 2-3 min recovery between
   - Proven to increase VO2max by 3-5%

2. **Billat 30/30** — Maximum density work
   - 30 seconds hard / 30 seconds easy × 15–30 reps
   - Higher total time at VO2max
   - Builds lactate tolerance simultaneously

3. **Rønnestad 30/15** — Fast-start effect
   - Harder at start, fatigue by end
   - 30s hard / 15s easy × ~30 reps
   - Neurological adaptation benefit

4. **40/20 Repeats** — Power development
   - 40 seconds hard / 20 seconds easy
   - Total time at VO2max
   - Builds sprint capacity too

**Result:** 4 different workouts, same goal, complete adaptation stimulus
